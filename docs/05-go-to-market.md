# AdVocate Go-to-Market Strategy

## Market Opportunity

### The Problem

**For Small Business Owners:**
- Paying agencies $1,500-5,000/month for basic ad management
- No visibility into what's actually working
- Leads come in, but can't prove which ads drove revenue
- Feel helpless and dependent on "experts" they can't verify

**For Agencies:**
- Manual work doesn't scale (same tasks repeated per client)
- Client churn from lack of visible results
- Racing to the bottom on pricing
- Spending hours on reporting instead of strategy

**For Everyone:**
- Google/Meta optimize for clicks, not revenue
- Massive gap between "conversions" and actual business results
- The businesses that win are the ones with better data feedback loops

### The Solution

AdVocate closes the loop: **real business outcomes feed directly back into ad optimization**.

When Google Ads knows that Lead A became a $15,000 customer and Lead B ghosted, it optimizes for more Lead As. That's the game. AdVocate plays it automatically.

## Pricing Strategy

### Model: SaaS + Managed Ad Spend

**Why not just SaaS subscription:**
- Aligns our success with client success
- Higher revenue potential as clients scale
- Natural expansion revenue

**Why not just % of ad spend:**
- Creates incentive to increase spend unnecessarily
- Hard to justify for low-spend clients

**Hybrid Approach:**
