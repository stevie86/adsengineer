# Beta Agency Recruitment & Launch

This guide covers recruiting and onboarding the first beta agencies for AdsEngineer.

## Beta Launch Strategy

### Target Profile
- **Agency Size**: 5-50 employees
- **Focus**: Digital marketing agencies using Google Ads/Facebook Ads
- **Pain Point**: Losing conversion data due to attribution issues
- **Budget**: $500-2,000/month marketing spend
- **Tech Savvy**: Comfortable with SaaS tools

### Value Proposition
"Stop losing 20-30% of your conversion data. AdsEngineer tracks leads across the entire funnel, ensuring every conversion is attributed correctly."

## Beta Onboarding Process

### Step 1: Discovery Call (15-30 minutes)
**Goals:**
- Understand their current tracking setup
- Identify pain points
- Qualify for beta fit
- Build relationship

**Questions:**
- What's your current Google Ads spend?
- How do you track conversions now?
- What's your biggest attribution challenge?
- What's your ideal conversion tracking solution?

### Step 2: Technical Assessment
**Send Questionnaire:**
- Current tech stack (CRM, website platform, etc.)
- Conversion goals and funnel steps
- Integration requirements
- Data security concerns

### Step 3: Beta Agreement
**Terms:**
- 3-month beta period
- 50% discount on first 3 months
- Priority support access
- Feedback sessions bi-weekly

### Step 4: Implementation
**Timeline:**
- **Week 1**: Setup call, API integration
- **Week 2**: Testing with live traffic
- **Week 3**: Optimization and training
- **Week 4**: Go-live and monitoring

### Step 5: Success Tracking
**Metrics:**
- Conversion tracking accuracy improvement
- Setup time vs. competitors
- Support ticket volume
- NPS score

- **Conversion**: 70% of invited agencies sign up for beta

- **Performance**: 95%+ uptime, <2s response times

- **Feedback**: NPS > 8.0
- **Referrals**: Beta users referring others
- **Case Studies**: 3+ success stories
- **Social Proof**: Testimonials and reviews

## Risk Mitigation

### Technical Risks
- **Downtime**: Have rollback plan and monitoring
- **Data Loss**: Regular backups, data validation
- **Security**: SOC2 compliance, regular audits

### Business Risks
- **Low Adoption**: Focus on high-intent prospects
- **Poor Fit**: Better qualification process
- **Competition**: Differentiate on ease of use
