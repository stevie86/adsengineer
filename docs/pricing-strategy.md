# AdsEngineer Pricing Strategy

**Target Market**: E-commerce stores spending $2K-50K/month on ads who want to know which campaigns actually convert

## Pricing Tiers

### Tier 1: Starter - $299/month
**Perfect for**: Stores doing $50K-200K/month revenue, spending $2K-10K on ads

**What they get**:
- 1 store connection (Shopify/WooCommerce/Custom)
- Up to 10K tracked events/month
- Basic conversion tracking (purchase, add_to_cart)
- GCLID & UTM parameter capture
- Real-time webhook processing
- Email support (24-48h response)
- Monthly conversion reports

**Value proposition**: "Know which Google Ads keywords actually convert to sales"

**ROI calculation**: If they save $500/month in wasted ad spend, it's profitable

---

### Tier 2: Growth - $799/month  
**Perfect for**: Stores doing $200K-1M/month revenue, spending $10K-50K on ads

**What they get**:
- 5 store connections
- Up to 100K tracked events/month
- Advanced conversion tracking (custom events, subscriptions)
- Customer lifetime value (LTV) tracking
- Multi-platform attribution (Google, Meta, TikTok)
- Priority support (4-12h response)
- Weekly conversion reports
- White-label reports for clients

**Value proposition**: "Prove marketing ROI to stakeholders with detailed attribution reports"

**ROI calculation**: If they optimize $2K/month in ad spend, it's profitable

---

### Tier 3: Enterprise - $2,499/month
**Perfect for**: Stores doing $1M+/month revenue, spending $50K+/month on ads

**What they get**:
- Unlimited store connections
- Unlimited tracked events
- Full SEO attribution intelligence (when launched)
- Customer LTV by marketing channel
- Revenue attribution by keyword (when launched)
- Dedicated account manager
- Same-day support response
- Daily conversion reports
- Custom integrations
- API access

**Value proposition**: "Complete marketing attribution intelligence platform for enterprise e-commerce"

**ROI calculation**: If they optimize $10K/month in ad spend, it's profitable

---

## Pricing Psychology

### Anchor Points
- **Google Analytics 360**: $150K/year ($12.5K/month) - makes $2.5K look cheap
- **Enterprise attribution tools**: $5K-50K/month - makes $799 look reasonable
- **Wasted ad spend**: 20-40% of budget - makes any price look good

### Value Metrics
- **Cost per conversion**: $0.01-0.05 per tracked conversion
- **Savings on ad spend**: 10-30% optimization typically achieved
- **Time saved**: 10-20 hours/month vs. manual attribution

### Annual vs Monthly
- **Monthly**: Default, easy to cancel
- **Annual**: 2 months free (17% discount) for committed customers
- **Setup fee**: None (remove friction)

## Competitive Positioning

### vs. Google Analytics 360 ($12.5K/month)
- **You**: Simple, transparent, e-commerce focused
- **Them**: Complex, confusing, generic analytics

### vs. Enterprise Attribution ($5K+/month)  
- **You**: Self-serve, 5-minute setup, WordPress plugin
- **Them**: 6-month implementation, consulting required

### vs. Facebook CAPI Tools ($500-2K/month)
- **You**: Multi-platform, WooCommerce native, transparent
- **Them**: Facebook-only, complex setup, black box
