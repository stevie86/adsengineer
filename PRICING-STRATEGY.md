# AdsEngineer Pricing Strategy

## Executive Summary

AdsEngineer is priced as a premium B2B SaaS solution for marketing agencies. Our value proposition centers on **eliminating 20-30% conversion data loss** through server-side tracking implementation. Pricing reflects the high ROI agencies achieve.

## Pricing Philosophy

**Value-Based Pricing:**
- Agencies lose 20-30% of conversion data with client-side tracking
- Average agency processes 1,000-10,000 leads/month
- Cost per lead: $50-500 depending on service
- Data loss = $10,000-50,000/month revenue impact

**Our Solution:**
- Restores full conversion visibility
- Enables data-driven optimization
- Reduces customer acquisition cost by 30-50%
